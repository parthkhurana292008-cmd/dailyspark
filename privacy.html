Section 04

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Daily Spark uses the following types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the website to function correctly (e.g., remembering your language preference between Hindi and English). You cannot opt out of these without affecting website functionality.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site — which articles are popular, how long people stay, and where they come from. This data is aggregated and anonymized. Google Analytics may set cookies such as _ga, _gid, and _gat.

Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense places cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history. These are third-party cookies managed by Google. See Section 5 for full details.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on Daily Spark. You may also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Section 05

Google AdSense & Advertising

Daily Spark uses Google AdSense, an advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense allows us to earn revenue to keep Daily Spark free for all users.

How AdSense Works

  • Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to Daily Spark and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits.
Opt-Out of Personalized Ads You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can visit www.aboutads.info to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising.

For more information about how Google uses data from sites that use its services, please visit: Google's Privacy & Terms.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Daily Spark is a general knowledge and news platform intended for users of all ages, including students. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 years without verifiable parental consent.

Our Parenting section and educational content may be accessed by or on behalf of children, but we do not collect any data specifically from children without parental oversight.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at dailyspark@contact.in. We will promptly delete such information from our records.

Safe for Students Daily Spark's educational content — including JoSAA/JAC counselling guides, government job listings, and study resources — is designed to be informative and safe for students of all ages.
Section 09

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Daily Spark implements the following measures:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted using HTTPS (SSL/TLS).
  • Limited Data Collection: We collect only the minimum data necessary for our services.
  • Access Controls: Access to any collected data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Review: We periodically review our data collection, storage, and processing practices.

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
