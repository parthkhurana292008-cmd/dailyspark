Tips for Parents

How to Make Math Fun at Home?

Mathematics is hidden in everyday chores. All you need is a fresh perspective!

1

Kitchen Counting

While cooking, ask your child: "There are 4 rotis and 6 people, how many more do we need?" That's practical arithmetic!

2

Market Estimates

When buying groceries, let your child estimate the total bill amount and verify it with the final receipt receipt.

3

Reading the Clock

Look at the clock and ask engaging situational questions: "There are 3 hours left of school, so what time will it end?"

4

License Plate Math

During road trips, add up digits on vehicle license plates or compete to spot the highest single number.

5

Praise Effort, Not Perfection

Celebrate every correct answer. Treat mistakes as learning opportunities rather than a cause for reprimand.
