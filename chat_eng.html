०१ — Definition

What is ChatGPT?

The most widely used AI chatbot, created by OpenAI

Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT)

ChatGPT is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) based chatbot created by OpenAI. It has learned from millions of books, websites, and research papers to converse like humans and answer questions.

Simply put — it is a Smart Virtual Assistant that understands your language and prepares information or content as per your needs. You can talk to ChatGPT by voice through the mobile app, giving a hands-free assistant experience. It is available 24/7, so students can study anytime and resolve their doubts.

03 — How It Works

ChatGPT How It Works?

In simple language, step by step

१

You type a question

Your question is sent to ChatGPT in written form.

→ इसे "Prompt" कहते हैं
२

ChatGPT understands the question

It breaks words into small pieces and understands the meaning of each piece.

→ These are called "Tokens"
३

The AI thinks

It searches through billions of parameters to find the right information. It prepares the most suitable answer based on statistical probability.

४

The answer appears

The answer is prepared word by word — that is why it appears to type slowly.

How was it trained?

1. Pre-training

Read millions of articles from the internet. Learned language patterns. Learned to predict the next word.

2. Fine-tuning

Humans indicated correct and incorrect answers. Good answers were rewarded, bad ones penalised.

3. Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF)

Humans ranked the answers. This helped the AI become better and better.

🎯 What is a Transformer?
ChatGPT का दिल ट्रांसफॉर्मर है — यह एक विशेष कृत्रिम बुद्धिमत्ता Architecture है
It sees the entire sentence at once, not one word at a time
It understands the relationship of each word with other words
💡 Example: "I ate a mango, it was very sweet" — here "sweet" refers to "mango". The Transformer understands this automatically.

⚠️ Limitations

Keep in Mind — This is Important to Know Too

Limited Internet Access

Doesn't always know real-time news — fresh information is only available in web search mode.

Information May Be Incorrect

Sometimes, due to incorrect information in training data, it may give wrong answers (Hallucinations). Always verify.

Knowledge Cutoff

Training stopped after a certain date — knowledge about very recent events may be incomplete.

No Real Understanding

It matches patterns — it doesn't truly think. It cannot replace a real expert.

⚡

The right approach: Use ChatGPT like a teacher, not a copying machine! Understand from it, then write yourself. Instead of depending on AI, learn from it and move forward.
