📜 Game Rules
  • 🎯 Math Answers = Move Each correct answer moves you 1-6 steps.
  • ⏱️ Time Limit You get only 15 seconds per question.
  • 🛡️ Safe Shield (Max 1) Get 1 Shield on 3 correct answers in a row! It protects from 1 snake bite. **You cannot hold more than 1 Shield.**
