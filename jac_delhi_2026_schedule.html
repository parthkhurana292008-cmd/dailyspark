JAC Delhi 2026 — Important Guidelines

Must Read
  • Fill the maximum number of choices/preferences during the registration window to maximize your chance of admission and upgradation through JAC Delhi.
  • Registration Fee of Rs. 1,500 is non-refundable under any circumstance — even if you do not get a seat or withdraw from counselling.
  • Choice Editing is available only during the registration windows — Window 1: May 28–June 9 and Window 2: June 26–28. No further choice editing will be allowed after this.
  • All category/sub-category certificates must be in the correct prescribed format when you report for document verification. No extra time will be given to produce missing certificates.
  • Seat Acceptance Fee payment starts immediately after result publication and closes at 2:00 PM on the last day of physical reporting of that round. Do not miss this deadline.
  • Candidates who have verified documents and received provisional admission letter are not required to report again to any university for subsequent rounds or upgradation.
  • A candidate already admitted who gets upgraded in the next round is NOT required to report at the upgraded university/institute — upgradation is automatic.
  • Candidates who wish to participate in the Spot Round must register during the regular registration window — there may be no fresh registrations before the Spot Round.

Withdrawal & Refund Policy

🚪
Withdrawal Deadline
Last date for withdrawal from all rounds is July 12, 2026 (Sunday) by 10:30 PM — only through candidate's JAC Delhi Portal login.
💰
Refund Policy
Refund of Seat Acceptance Fee is applicable only if withdrawal is done on or before July 12, 2026, 10:30 PM. No refund is admissible after this.
📧
Withdrawal Mode
Withdrawal only through online portal login. Requests via written application or email will NOT be considered under any circumstance.
⚠️
After Withdrawal
Withdrawn candidates exit all remaining rounds. They may participate only in the Spot Round if conducted. The decision is permanent.
