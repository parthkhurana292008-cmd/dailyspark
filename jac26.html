Frequently Asked Questions — JAC Delhi 2026 15 FAQs
1
What exactly is JAC Delhi and which colleges does it cover?
▼
JAC Delhi is the centralized counselling authority for B.Tech admissions in Delhi's top government engineering colleges through JEE Main scores.

🔵 DTU — Delhi Technological University
🔴 NSUT — Netaji Subhas University of Technology
🟢 IIIT-Delhi — Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology
🟣 DSEU — Delhi Skill and Entrepreneurship University
🟠 IGDTUW — Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (female only)

All offer 4-year B.Tech programs and are among the most sought-after engineering institutions in India.
2
What does "cutoff rank" mean and how is it different from a merit rank?
▼
A cutoff rank is the JEE Main rank of the last candidate allotted a seat in a particular college, branch, category, and round.

⚠️ Critical: A lower rank number = stronger candidate. Rank 5,000 is better than rank 15,000. If DTU CSE General Round 5 cutoff is 11,352 — anyone with rank 1 to 11,352 was eligible. If your rank is below (better than) the cutoff, you qualify. If above, you don't — for that combination in that round.
3
Why do cutoffs relax from Round 1 to Round 5?
▼
In Round 1, top-ranked students secure first-choice seats. In Round 2–3, many upgrade to better options — vacating earlier seats. Those seats re-open to candidates with slightly weaker ranks.

📌 Don't give up if your rank just misses Round 1. Thousands of students every year get seats in Round 4 or 5 that were impossible in Round 1. The Spot Round after Round 5 offers the most relaxed cutoffs of the entire process.
4
What is the difference between Delhi domicile and Outside Delhi seats?
▼
JAC Delhi reserves ~85% seats for Delhi domicile candidates and ~15% for Outside Delhi. Outside Delhi candidates compete nationally, so their cutoffs are much tougher.

📊 DTU CSE General Round 5 (2025):
🟢 Delhi: ~11,352  |  🔴 Outside Delhi: ~6,200

✅ Delhi domicile = massive advantage. Outside Delhi candidates should simultaneously explore JoSAA for NITs and IIITs.
5
Which branches are the toughest to get into across JAC Delhi colleges?
▼
2025 Round 5 General (Delhi) cutoffs:

🔴 NSUT CSAI — ~7,946 (toughest overall)
🔴 IIIT-Delhi CSE — ~8,200
🔴 DTU CSE — ~11,352
🔴 NSUT CSE — ~12,500
🟡 DTU MAC — ~14,000 (excellent alternative)
🟡 DTU ECE — ~22,000 (strong, far more accessible)

💡 If your rank misses CSE, ECE and MAC have nearly identical first-year curriculum and comparable tech placements.
6
How should I strategically use cutoff data to predict my 2026 chances?
▼
3-step strategy:

Step 1 — Safety buffer: If your rank is 10–20% better than the Round 5 cutoff, you have a strong chance.

Step 2 — Layered choices: Fill 10–15 choices — 3 aspirational, 5–6 realistic, 3–4 safe backups.

Step 3 — Track trends: If a branch tightened 3 years in a row, expect it to tighten again. Use the charts on this page to identify patterns.
7
What is CSAI at NSUT and why is it so competitive?
▼
CSAI = Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence — the toughest branch in all of JAC Delhi. Why?

1️⃣ Extremely limited seats
2️⃣ Massive demand — AI is the hottest field in 2026
3️⃣ Outstanding placements — Google, Microsoft, top MNCs at premium packages

💡 If CSAI is out of reach, NSUT CSE or DTU ECE are equally strong — same top recruiters visit both.
8
Is IGDTUW only for female candidates? How does it compare in cutoffs?
▼
Yes — IGDTUW is exclusively for female candidates, creating a huge strategic advantage:

✅ Cutoffs 15,000–25,000 ranks more relaxed than DTU/NSUT for the same branch
✅ Rank ~20,000 who misses DTU CSE can comfortably get CSE at IGDTUW
✅ Excellent placements — Microsoft, Deloitte, Infosys, top startups
✅ Women-specific scholarships and mentorship programs

📌 Every female candidate should add IGDTUW CSE/ECE as a top-priority choice.
9
What is the MAC branch at DTU — is it worth choosing?
▼
MAC = Mathematics and Computing — DTU's most underrated yet powerful branch.

📚 Curriculum: Advanced maths, statistics, data science, algorithms, ML — heavy on logic and programming.

💼 Recruiters: Google, Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, D.E. Shaw — same as CSE.

📊 Cutoff only 2,000–5,000 ranks more relaxed than DTU CSE. With Delhi General rank 13,000–17,000, MAC at DTU often outperforms CSE at tier-2 private colleges in placements and alumni network.
10
What reservation categories are available and how do they affect cutoffs?
▼
JAC Delhi 2026 categories:

🔵 GN — Unreserved; toughest cutoffs
🟠 OBC — 27% reservation; ~3,000–10,000 ranks more relaxed than GN
🟣 SC — 15% reservation; significantly more relaxed
🟢 ST — 7.5% reservation; most relaxed
🟡 EWS — 10% for GN with family income below ₹8 lakh/year

✅ Always apply under your correct category — your competition pool shrinks dramatically. Ensure certificate is valid before registration deadline.
11
My rank is higher than the Round 5 cutoff — is there still hope?
▼
Yes — 5 real options remain:

1️⃣ 2026 cutoffs may relax — tougher paper = fewer good ranks = easier cutoffs (5,000–15,000 rank difference possible)
2️⃣ Adjacent branches — ECE, IT, SE, or MAC may be within range with comparable placements
3️⃣ Spot Round — most relaxed cutoffs of the entire JAC process
4️⃣ East campuses — DTU East and NSUT East have notably more relaxed cutoffs
5️⃣ Register anyway — you can decline but cannot apply after the deadline closes
12
Which branches show the most cutoff relaxation from Round 1 to Round 5?
▼
Branches where later rounds help most (General, Delhi, 2025):

🏗️ Civil Engineering — 15,000–25,000 rank relaxation
⚙️ Mechanical Engineering — 10,000–20,000 rank relaxation
🌿 Environmental Engineering — 20,000+ rank relaxation
🧬 Biotechnology — 15,000–20,000 rank relaxation

📌 Rank between 50,000–90,000? These branches at DTU/NSUT are achievable in Round 3–4 and offer strong PSU and core industry career paths.
13
DTU vs NSUT — which is better for placements and overall experience?
▼
🏛️ DTU: Stronger national brand, slightly higher average CTC in CSE/ECE, larger senior alumni network, more active research culture.

🏛️ NSUT: Rapidly improving placements, NSUT CSAI has Delhi's highest packages, strong software and AI industry connections.

🎯 Verdict: DTU holds a slight overall edge for the same branch. But branch matters far more than college — CSE at NSUT consistently outperforms Mechanical at DTU in salaries and job quality.

What documents do I need for JAC Delhi 2026 counselling and reporting?
▼
Keep originals + 1 self-attested photocopy of each:

📄 JEE Main 2026 Admit Card and Score Card
📄 Class X certificate (date of birth proof)
📄 Class XII marksheet and admit card
📄 Seat Acceptance Fee payment receipt
📄 Online registration form (signed by candidate and parent)
📄 Seat allotment letter from JAC portal
📄 3 recent passport-size photographs
📄 Valid category certificate (OBC/SC/ST/EWS — if applicable)
📄 Medical fitness certificate in prescribed format
📄 Delhi domicile certificate (Delhi region candidates)

✅ Prepare a dedicated folder before reporting day — one missing document can delay your admission.
15
What is the single most important advice for JAC Delhi 2026 aspirants?
▼
Fill as many choices as possible — and fill them wisely.

Most students make two mistakes: too few choices and ranking emotionally instead of strategically.

✅ Fill 15–20 choices minimum across colleges and branches
✅ Rank by genuine preference, not by cutoff — the system allots your best qualifying option automatically
✅ Include 3–4 safe choices where your rank is comfortably within Round 3 cutoff
✅ Participate in every round — upgrading is free
✅ Never freeze prematurely unless 100% satisfied

The JAC process rewards candidates who are thorough, patient, and strategic. One good decision in choice-filling can change your entire career trajectory.
