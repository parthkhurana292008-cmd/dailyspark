01 — Definition

What is ChatGPT?

The world's most widely used AI chatbot, built by OpenAI

Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT)

ChatGPT is an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-based chatbot built by OpenAI. It has learned from hundreds of millions of books, websites and research papers to converse like a human and answer any question.

Simply put — it is a Smart Virtual Assistant that understands your language and creates information or content as per your needs. You can even speak to ChatGPT through the mobile app, making it a hands-free virtual assistant experience.

03 — How It Works

ChatGPT How It Works?

In simple terms, step by step

1

You Type a Question

Your question is sent to ChatGPT in written form.

→ This is called "Prompts (Prompt)"
2

ChatGPT Understands the Question

It breaks words into small pieces and understands the meaning of each piece.

→ These are called "Tokens"
3

The AI Thinks

It searches through billions of parameters to find the right information and prepares the most suitable answer using statistical probability.

4

The Answer Appears

The answer is generated word by word — that is why it appears to type slowly.

How Was It Trained?

1. Pre-training

Read hundreds of millions of articles from the internet. Learned language patterns. Learned to predict the next word.

2. Fine-tuning

Humans labelled right and wrong answers. Good answers were rewarded, bad ones penalised.

3. Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF)

Humans ranked responses. The AI improved further from this feedback.

Technical Architecture

Architecture Transformer Neural Network
Parameters Billions
Training Data Vast portion of the internet
Output Generates one word at a time

    📚 Study — Physics
    Newton ka law batao
    What level? Which language? JEE or Class 9?
    ✅ Good Prompt
    📚 Study — Physics
    Explain Newton's three laws of motion in English, give one real-life example for each law, and also create 2 JEE-level questions

    ❌ Bad Prompt
    ✍️ Writing — Essay
    Essay likho
    Which topic? How long? For whom?
    ✅ Good Prompt

    Topic, length, grade and structure — all specified!
    ❌ Bad Prompt
    💻 Coding — Python
    Code banao
    Which language? What to build? What level?
    ✅ Good Prompt

    Language, features, level and format — perfect!

    Destination, duration, budget, expectations — crystal clear!

    🎯 Transformer?
    The heart of ChatGPT is the Transformer — a special AI architecture
    It sees the entire sentence at once, not word by word
    It understands the relationship between every word
    💡 Example: "I ate a mango, it was very sweet" — here "sweet" is connected to "mango". The Transformer understands this automatically.

    ⚠️ Limitations

    Keep in Mind — This is Important to Know

    Limited Internet Access

    Doesn't always know real-time news — fresh information is only available in web search mode.

    Can Give Incorrect Information

    Sometimes it may give incorrect information due to errors in training data (Hallucinations). Always verify.

    Knowledge Cut-off Date

    Training stopped at a certain date — information about very recent events may be incomplete.

    No Real Understanding

    It matches patterns — it doesn't truly think. It cannot replace a real expert.
